This website presents results from an ongoing living systematic review and meta-analysis evaluating the survival impact of combining radiotherapy and immunotherapy across solid tumors. Randomized trials published between January 2010 and January 2026 were identified through systematic searches of PubMed and EMBASE, examining both the addition of immunotherapy to a radiotherapy backbone and the addition of radiotherapy to an immunotherapy backbone. Survival outcomes including overall survival (OS), progression-free survival (PFS), and event-free survival (EFS) were pooled using random-effects meta-analysis with risk of bias assessed using the RoB-2 tool.
